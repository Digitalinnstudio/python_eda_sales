# Product Performance & Discount Impact Analysis

## Overview
This project performs EDA on sales data to uncover insights about product performance, discount strategies, and profitability. The analysis helps inform business decisions related to pricing, marketing, and inventory management.

---

## Business Context
organisation requested an analysis to understand
- Which products drive the most sales, profit, and volume.
- How discount levels affect both sales volume and profit margins.

By analysing historical sales data, we identified clear trends across product categories and discount levels that directly influence revenue and profitability.

### Key Insights
- **High Sales vs. High Quantity Discrepancy:**  
  No overlap between the top selling (by revenue) and top selling (by quantity) products. This highlights two distinct business strategies:
  - High value, low volume premium goods.  
  - Low value, high volume essentials.

- **Sales & Profit Alignment:**  
  Products like the Canon Copier and premium smartphones appear in both top sales and top profit lists showing they are reliable revenue and profit drivers.

- **High-Margin Niche Products:**  
  Hoover Stove and Sauder Bookcase deliver strong profits despite modest sales volume ideal for focused marketing.


**Conclusion:**  
The business relies heavily on high value electronics and furniture for profitability, while office essentials sustain steady sales volume. A balanced strategy across these segments is key for growth.

## Strategic Insights
- Focus marketing efforts on high margin, high revenue products (e.g., premium electronics, furniture).  
- Maintain low volume essentials as steady cash flow contributors.  
- Implement controlled discountin target small to moderate reductions for maximum profitability.  
- Align inventory and promotions with seasonal sales trends (Q4 peaks, Q1 dips).
